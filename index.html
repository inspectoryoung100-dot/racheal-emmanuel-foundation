Our Core Purpose

Vision and Aims of the Foundation

The Rachael Emmanuel Foundation is driven by a deep commitment to compassion and community welfare. Our vision is to create a nurturing environment where the most vulnerable members of society can find support, stability, and opportunity. We believe that every individual, regardless of their circumstances, deserves to live a healthy, dignified, and empowered life. Our programs are designed to address immediate needs while fostering long-term resilience and independence.

Our Guiding Principle:

"To help and empower the needy and orphans, and to empower the widow women, ensuring they are not forgotten by society."

Impact

Six Pillars of Our Community Support

Our programs target critical areas of need to maximize positive and lasting change.

Empowering the Needy & Orphans

We provide essential resources, shelter, education, and emotional support to vulnerable children and families in desperate need.

Widow Empowerment

Our programs equip widow women with skills and financial literacy to achieve economic stability and regain independence.

Free Blood Pressure Screening

Regular, accessible health checks are vital. We offer free blood pressure monitoring to manage hypertension risks.

Free Blood Sugar Level Check

We provide essential screenings for blood glucose levels, helping to detect and manage diabetes in the community.

Eye Exams & Free Eyeglasses

Restoring sight and preventing blindness by offering free eye examinations and distributing needed corrective eyeglasses.

Children's Deworming Program

Focusing on preventative health for children aged 2-16, this program ensures better growth and improved immunity.
